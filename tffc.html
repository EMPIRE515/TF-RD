The TFFC Difference

Why The Forest Holds the Key to Beauty

Biological Affinity

Unlike synthetic oils that sit on the surface, forest fats like Moabi Butter mimic your skin’s natural sebum. This allows for deep cellular absorption without clogging pores.

Nature’s Laboratory

Wild-harvested ingredients are "stressed" by their environment, leading them to produce 10x more antioxidants than farm-grown plants. This provides a superior shield against urban pollution.

Zero Endocrine Disruptors

Most commercial cosmetics contain parabens that mimic hormones. Our forest products are 100% "Live"—meaning they are free from preservatives that interfere with your body's chemistry.

Preserving the Source

Every bottle of Njansang Oil makes the standing forest more valuable than its timber. You aren't just buying a product; you are funding the protection of the Dja Biosphere Reserve.
